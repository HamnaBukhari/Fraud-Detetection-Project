How to Use the Fraud Detection System

This system helps you to detect potential financial frauds by analyzing the data from your CSV file. Follow these steps to use the system:

  1. Upload Your Data: Click on the "Choose File" button on the Home page, and select your CSV file. Make sure that the file follows the correct format. You can download a sample input file to see an example of the correct format.
  2. Check for Fraud: After you've selected your file, click on the "Upload and Check for Fraud" button. The system will then process your file and analyze the data for potential frauds.
  3. View Results: Once the processing is complete, you will see the results displayed on the page. The results will show you the transactions that are flagged as potential frauds, along with their details.

If you have any further questions or need additional assistance, please contact our support team.
